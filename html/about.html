About Me

Living the American Dream.

I was born in San Jose, California and grew up all over the country. After stops in Colorado, Arizona, Louisiana, Nevada, and Texas, my family settled in Indiana, where I attended Zionsville Community High School.

After graduating high school, I enrolled at BYU, where I planned to study Mechanical Engineering. I attended classes for a year before deciding to serve a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. I was assigned to serve in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. I served my whole mission as the financial secretary before returning home early due to health concerns.

After returning home, I was accepted into the Mechanical Engineering program at BYU. After another year of Mechanical Engineering courses, I decided it was not the best career path for me. I took a full year off of school to work full-time. During my gap year, I met Grace. Grace and I have dated for 14 and a half months, and we recently got engaged. We plan to be married on August 13th! After returning to school, I decided to enroll in business prerequesite courses, and am preparing to apply to the Finance, Strategy, and Global Supply Chain Management programs at BYU.

In my free time, I enjoy playing soccer, basketball, or any other sport. I love attending BYU sporting events and being outdoors. I love to socialize and meet new people, and I love spending time with my fiancée.
